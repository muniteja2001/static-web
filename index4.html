Pricing

what is pricing ?

Pricing is the process of determining the monetary value that a company will charge customers for a product or service. It's a fundamental part of product management and one of the four Ps of marketing, along with product, promotion, and place.

how pricing is used ?

Pricing is the process of setting the price at which a business sells its products and services. It's a fundamental part of product management and one of the four Ps of the marketing mix, along with product, promotion, and place. Pricing is important because it's a strategic tool that can create customer value and lead to revenues

types of pricing ?

Pricing strategies are the different approaches that businesses take to figure out what the cost of their goods and services should be. To choose the appropriate pricing strategy, companies consider factors like current product demand, cost of goods sold, consumer behavior, and market conditions.
